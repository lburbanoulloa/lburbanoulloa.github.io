About me

Who is Luis Valle?

From an early age, Luis has been interested in various forms of artistic expression. It was during his Psychology studies at the University of Granada that he discovered a passion for theatre and movement. From then on, his path took a turn towards the stage, focused on aerial disciplines and the multidisciplinary vision that contemporary circus provides.

His training includes studies at "Die Etage" (school of circus and performing arts, Berlin), and a degree at "Carampa" (school of circus, Madrid) where he specialised in a deepening of artistic and technical skills. Parallel to performing, he also delivers regular group classes, private tuition, and intensive courses.

Luis has received training in aerial techniques from Friederike Krahl, Faon Shane, Maxime Yelle, Francisco Loyola, Zenaida Alcalde, Emiliano Ron, Carolina Bustamante and Maria Mateo, amongst others. This has led him to develop a dynamic and adaptive movement language of his own. This unique style continues to evolve due to his constant pursuit and development, and is complemented by a powerful stage presence. He continues to further enhance his discipline with dance and acting projects.

Technical requirements

Technical requirements

  • -One anchoring point (two in the case of fixed trapeze) that can support at least 300kg.
  • -Minimum stage size: 5m long x 5m wide x 5m high.
  • -Lighting: at least one spotlight that lights the whole aparatus.
  • -Sound: MP3/USB player
